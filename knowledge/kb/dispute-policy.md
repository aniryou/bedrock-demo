# Dispute and Cancellation Policy

A customer may dispute an order while it is OPEN or SHIPPED.

- OPEN disputed orders should be flagged for human review with the reason "customer dispute".
- SHIPPED orders enter the returns process and are not re-flagged by triage.
- CANCELLED and CLOSED orders are final; disputes on them are handled by finance, not triage.

Refunds above $50,000 require finance approval in addition to triage review.
