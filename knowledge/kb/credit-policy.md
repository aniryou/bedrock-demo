## Standard terms and limits

Customer credit is extended on standard payment terms not exceeding 90 days.
Limits are set per customer based on the assigned tier and trading history, and
reviewed at least annually.

## Collateral for high-risk markets

Where exposure is concentrated in a high-risk market, collateral is required
before any limit increase takes effect. The required cover scales with the
outstanding exposure and the market's risk classification.

## Committee override process

Requests above the approved limit, or new credit following a recent default, are
declined by default and may only proceed via the credit committee. The committee
records the rationale and any compensating conditions (for example, collateral or
a shortened term).
