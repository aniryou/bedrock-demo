# Order Review Policy

Human review is required before an OPEN order proceeds when any of the following hold:

- The computed risk score for the order is **high**.
- The order is placed through the **partner** channel and the amount is at least $100,000.
- The customer is on a credit hold in SAP.

Only OPEN orders can be flagged for review. Orders that are SHIPPED, CLOSED, or CANCELLED
are out of scope for flagging — escalate through the exceptions process instead.

A reviewer SLA of one business day applies once an order is flagged.
